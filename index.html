About Me

I am recent graduate of Clemson University where I earned a Master's of Science in Data Science and Analytics. While working hard in this program, I have completed some of my first Data Science/Analytics projects, allowing me to expand my knowledge in the Data Science field.

Before studying at Clemson, I attended a small school in Clinton, South Carolina called Presbyterian College, where I majored in Applied Mathematics with minors in Business Administration and Physics. While attending PC, I spent four years playing on the Men's Soccer team while also being involved in Greek Life as well as associations such as the Math Honors Society, Honor Council, and Residence Life. Also, while studying at Presbyterian College, I was able to travel to England, where I completed a study abroad program in May of 2019.

One of my favorite things to do is travel and see the world. This has led me to many different states and countries including Mexico, Costa Rica, England and Ireland. When I am not traveling, I like to play sports such as golf and soccer, spend time with my friends and family, and continually learn.
